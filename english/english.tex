\section{Let us Pray}

God our Father,
 as we have celebrated today the mystery of the Lord’s resurrection,
 grant our humble prayer:
free us from all harm
 that we may sleep in peace
 and rise in joy to sing your praise.
Through Christ our Lord,
Amen.
